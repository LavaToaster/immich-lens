**Immich Lens — Privacy Policy**

*Last updated: 3 March 2026*

**Overview**

Immich Lens is a client for Immich, the self-hosted photo and video management platform. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is designed. Immich Lens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

**Data Collection**

Immich Lens does not collect any data. Specifically:

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting sent to third parties
- No advertising or ad frameworks
- No account creation required
- No cookies or device identifiers collected

**Network Communication**

Immich Lens communicates solely with the Immich server you configure. All network requests are made directly between the app and your server. No data is sent to the developer or any third party.

**Server Credentials**

Your Immich server URL and API key are stored securely on your device using the Keychain. These credentials are never transmitted to anyone other than your own server.

**Photos and Videos**

All photos and videos displayed in the app are fetched directly from your self-hosted Immich server. Immich Lens does not cache, upload, or share your media with any external service.

**Third-Party Services**

Immich Lens does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data.

**Children's Privacy**

Immich Lens does not collect any data from any user, including children.

**Changes to This Policy**

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date. As Immich Lens does not collect any data, there is no means of notifying users directly.

**Contact**

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/LavaToaster/immich-lens/issues
